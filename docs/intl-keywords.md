# Peptips — International keyword research (DE/FR/IT/ES/NL/PL/SV)

**For:** peptips.com · **Source:** Semrush, broad-match "ozempic" per market, 2026-06-03

> **Key insight:** non-EN markets search very differently from the US. The big clusters everywhere are **side effects, "what is it / how it works," price/cost, and weight-loss** — *not* the supplement-affiliate or fine-grained comparison terms built for EN. The "best electrolytes/protein for Ozempic" affiliate model barely exists in these languages. So these locales should be **informational/ad-supported, not affiliate**: translate the education/side-effect/dosing posts, skip the money pages.

## Where the opportunity is (cross-market)
1. **Side-effect content** — safest, highest-volume translate-and-rank play in every market.
2. **"Ozempic face"** — massive and we don't even have an EN post yet (DE 32k, NL 10k, FR 4.4k). Fill in EN *and* localize.
3. **Price / cost / reimbursement** — far bigger abroad than the US (PL 49.5k, DE/ES ~27k). Big traffic, but *not monetizable* (we don't sell drugs).
4. **Localized safety scares** — DE cancer, ES/SE eye-disease (NAION), NL "is it dangerous." Rankable informational.
5. **"Without a prescription / where to buy"** is huge (NL, DE, PL, ES) — answer *informationally* only, never sell or link vendors.
6. **Supply/stock tracking** uniquely big in **Sweden**.

## Recommendation (per non-EN locale)
Translate, in priority order:
1. Side-effect guide + symptom posts (nausea, fatigue, diarrhea, hair loss; alcohol where demand exists — NL, SE)
2. "What is Ozempic / how it works" (beginner guide)
3. Dosing schedule pillar
4. A weight-loss explainer
5. NEW: an "Ozempic face" post (add to EN too)
6. A country-specific price / reimbursement explainer

**Skip** translating the supplement affiliate pages (electrolytes/fiber/protein) — near-zero local volume. Keep all "where to buy / without prescription" content strictly informational.
