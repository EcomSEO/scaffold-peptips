## Affiliate engine
- Cloaked `/go/` redirect links with proper `rel="sponsored nofollow"`
- 28 real, verified products with brand-original photos
- Per-product pages auto-generated; product comparison cards with buy buttons (44px mobile tap targets)
- **No** telehealth, peptide-vendor, or weight-loss-supplement links (clean and compliant)

## Trust & compliance (E-E-A-T)
- Honest editorial framing — **no fabricated doctors or fake "medically reviewed" claims** (removed entirely); content is checked against FDA labels & trials
- Medical disclaimers, no weight-loss numbers, generic + brand drug names throughout — YMYL-safe

## SEO & technical
- Unique titles + meta descriptions on every page
- Valid structured data (Article, MedicalWebPage, FAQ, Product, Breadcrumb) — eligible for rich results
- **Social/OG images on every page** (each post pins with its own image — Pinterest-ready)
- Internal linking throughout, sitemap, canonical tags
- Indexing locked to peptips.com only (duplicate hosts excluded)
- Keyword strategy grounded in live Semrush data (symptom + comparison + dosing intent)
